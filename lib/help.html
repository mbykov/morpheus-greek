Just select some Ancient Greek sentence anywhere at the decktop and copy it: Ctrl-C

 

Esc, [x] - hide window

ctrl-Q - close program completely

ctrl-P - open Perseus WST in your default browser with this wordform

ctrl-L - open paradigm of this wordform on lexigram.gr in your default browser

 

note: bare wordform and wordform in a sentence have the different morphologies

- upper: all forms of pronouns, particles, conjunctions, irregular verbs, etc

- small short educational dictionary

- Liddell-Scott-Jones
