Morpheus - automation of the process of reading the ancient text. That is, flipping through the dictionaries, digging in grammatical tables, etc. But is not scientific linguistic program - understanding is your action.

http://gr.diglossa.org

Morpheus is designed in accordance with the grammars of Aristotle, Dionysius Thax, Apollonius Dyscolus, rather then the modern grammar.

Morpheus - an an open source project released under the GNU GPL lisence
